Buy GO! Solutions Pet Food in Mantua, NJ

A lot of "premium" food asks you to pick a bag and hope it fits your pet. GO! Solutions flips that: it sorts its recipes by the problem you're actually trying to solve. Dull coat? Sensitive stomach? Carrying a few extra pounds? There's a line built for each. That match-the-recipe-to-the-need approach is exactly how we like to think about food here on Bridgeton Pike, which is why GO! earns a spot on our shelves.

It comes from Petcurean, a Canadian-owned company that's been making premium pet food for over 20 years. Their philosophy is refreshingly un-trendy: recipes are formulated for ingredient bioavailability — how well your pet's body can actually absorb and use what's in the bowl — rather than chasing the latest ingredient trends. That work is led by a real nutrition team, including Nutrition Manager Natalie Williams (M.Sc. in Companion Animal Nutrition) and nutritionists with advanced degrees from the University of Guelph and the University of Alberta. They also take sustainability seriously: responsible sourcing, recyclable packaging, and support for local farmers.

Here's where the "Solutions" name earns its keep. The line is organized into targeted recipes, so you can feed toward a specific goal:

  • Skin + Coat Care — for dry, flaky skin or a dull coat; omega fatty acids from salmon oil and flaxseed support a shiny, healthy coat.
  • Sensitivities — limited-ingredient recipes with a single animal-protein source and minimal ingredients, formulated to reduce common allergy triggers.
  • Weight Management + Joint Care — balanced for a healthy weight, with glucosamine and chondroitin to support mobility.
  • Digestive Health — prebiotics, probiotics, and pumpkin to support a healthy gut and smooth digestion.

The honest part: "targeted" doesn't mean "diagnostic." These recipes are formulated to support skin, digestion, weight, or joints — they're nutrition, not medicine. If your pet has a diagnosed condition, we'll always loop in your vet. But for everyday "my dog's coat could be shinier" or "my cat's stomach is a little touchy," matching the right GO! recipe is often a smart, simple first move.

Who GO! Solutions is a good fit for

GO! works well for pet parents who know roughly what they're aiming for — a calmer gut, a glossier coat, a lighter frame, easier joints — and want a recipe built around that goal instead of a one-size-fits-all bag.
