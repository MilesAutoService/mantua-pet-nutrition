What makes Fromm worth a look? Start with the basics done right. Every formula is built with the proper proportions of protein, fat, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals — the stuff that supports muscle, energy, immune function, and a sharp mind. The proteins are premium meats like chicken, beef, lamb, and fish, chosen because they're highly digestible and rich in the amino acids dogs actually use. Healthy fats from chicken fat and salmon oil bring omega-3s and omega-6s for skin, coat, and joint support. And there are real extras in there too — probiotics and digestive enzymes for gut balance, plus antioxidant-rich blueberries, cranberries, and spinach to support immunity and longevity.

The trust piece is just as important. Fromm runs stringent quality control with regular testing and facility inspections, and the family business has never had a product recall — a track record that says a lot in a category where recalls aren't rare. Every ingredient is traceable to its source, so "what's really in this bag?" has an actual answer.

We also like Fromm because it meets your dog where they are. There are tailored formulas for life stages and needs — Puppy Gold for the little ones, Senior Gold for the gray-muzzle crew, and Limited Ingredient Diets for dogs with allergies or sensitive stomachs. Grain-friendly recipes use brown rice, barley, and oatmeal for fiber and steady energy; grain-free options lean on sweet potatoes and peas for dogs that do better without grain. Not sure which lane fits your dog? That's a two-minute conversation with one of our Holistic Pet Food Experts.

Has Fromm ever been recalled?

Fromm reports it has never had a product recall, backed by regular testing and facility inspections. That consistency is a big part of why we carry it.

Does Fromm make puppy and senior formulas?

Yes — Puppy Gold, Large Breed Puppy Gold, and Senior Gold are built for those life stages, alongside adult and Limited Ingredient Diet recipes.
